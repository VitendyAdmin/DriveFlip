DriveFlip — 3-Pane Layout

Drives | Details | Actions — everything visible, everything scrolls independently.

IDLE STATE

Left pane is a narrow drive list — just enough for model name, size, risk badge.
Center pane scrolls vertically with all sections stacked: Info → Health → SMART. No tabs, no height cap.
Right pane has the three action cards with collapsible wipe options. Copy for Listing and Dump Raw Data buttons move into the center pane's Info section header.
